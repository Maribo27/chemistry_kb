• Copper oxide minerals (cuprite)
  • Aluminum oxide and hydroxide minerals (Corundum, diaspora, bumite, hydrargillite, bauxite, spinel)
  • Iron oxide and hydroxide minerals, titanium and chromium oxide minerals (hematite, magnetite, goethite, lepidocrocite, limonite, rutile, ilmenite, chromite)
