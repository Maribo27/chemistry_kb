Oxides are complex substances, which contain atoms of two chemical elements, one of which is oxygen.
Classification:

  • Unsalt forming oxides - CO, N2O, NO.
  • Salt forming oxides:
    • Basic
      - are metal oxides in which the latter exhibit a slight oxidation state of +1, +2
      Na2O; MgO; CuO
    • Amphoteric
      (usually for metals with an oxidation state of +3, +4). As hydrates, they correspond to amphoteric hydroxides
      ZnO; Al2O3; Cr2O3; SnO2
    • Acidic
      - are oxides of non-metals and metals with oxidation degree from +5 to +7
      SO2; SO3; P2O5; Mn2O7; CrO3.
The basic oxides correspond to bases, acid - acids, amphoteric - both of them

Receiving

  1. Interaction of simple and complex substances with oxygen:
    • 2Mg + O2 -> 2MgO
    • 4P + 5O2 -> 2P2O5
    • S + O2 -> SO2
    • 2CuS + 3O2 -> 2CuO + 2SO2
    • CH4 + 2O2 -> CO2 + 2H2O
    • 4NH3 + 5O2 –кат.-> 4NO + 6H2O
  2. Decomposition of some oxygen-containing substances (bases, acids, salts) with heating:
    • Cu(OH)2 –t°-> CuO + H2O
    • (CuOH)2CO3 –t°-> 2CuO + CO2 + H2O
    • 2Pb(NO3)2 –t°® 2PbO + 4NO2 + O2
    • 2HMnO4 –t°;H2SO4(конц.)-> Mn2O7 + H2O
Chemical properties
  1. Interaction with water
    • Basic oxides formed base:
      • Na2O + H2O -> 2NaOH
      • CaO + H2O -> Ca(OH)2
    • Acidic oxides formed acid:
      • SO3 + H2O -> H2SO4
      • P2O5 + 3H2O -> 2H3PO4
  2. Interaction with acid or base:
    • Basic oxides - in the reaction with acid formed salt and water:
      • MgO + H2SO4 –t°-> MgSO4 + H2O
      • CuO + 2HCl –t°-> CuCl2 + H2O
    • Acidic oxides - in the reaction with the base formed salt and water:
      • CO2 + Ba(OH)2 -> BaCO3 + H2O
      • SO2 + 2NaOH -> Na2SO3 + H2O
    • Amphoteric oxides interact with acids as basic:
      • ZnO + H2SO4 -> ZnSO4 + H2O
    • with bases as acidic:
      • ZnO + 2NaOH -> Na2ZnO2 + H2O
      • (ZnO + 2NaOH + H2O -> Na2[Zn(OH)4])
  3. Interaction of basic and acidic oxides leads to salts.
    • Na2O + CO2 -> Na2CO3
  4. Recovery to simple substances:
    • 3CuO + 2NH3 -> 3Cu + N2 + 3H2O
    • P2O5 + 5C -> 2P + 5CO
