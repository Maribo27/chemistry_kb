Oxides are complex substances, which contain atoms of two chemical elements, one of which is oxygen.
Classification:

  • Unsalt forming oxides - CO, N2O, NO.
  • Salt forming oxides:
    • Basic
    • Amphoteric
    • Acidic

Receiving

  1. Interaction of simple and complex substances with oxygen:
    • 2CO + O2 -> 2CO2
  2. Decomposition of some oxygen-containing substances (bases, acids, salts) with heating:
    • (CuOH)2CO3 –t°-> 2CuO + CO2 + H2O
    • 2Pb(NO3)2 –t°® 2PbO + 4NO2 + O2
