The basic hydroxides in the aqueous solution dissociate to form anions of OH- and cations.
The name of the base usually consists of two words: the words "hydroxide" and the name of the metal in the genitive case.

Getting

  • Interaction of strong basic oxide with water allows to obtain a strong base or alkali.
    CaO + H2O -> Ca(OH)2
  • The base can also be obtained by reacting an alkaline or alkaline earth metal with water.
    Ca + 2H2O -> Ca(OH)2 + H2
  • Alkali metal hydroxides in industry are obtained by electrolysis of aqueous solutions of salts:
    2NaCl + 2H2O -> 2NaOH + H2 + Cl2
  • Some reasons can be obtained by exchange reactions:
    Li 2SO 4 + Ba (OH)2 -> 2LiOH + BaSO4

The foundations of metals occur naturally in the form of minerals, for example: hydrargillite Al (OH) 3, brucite Mg (OH) 2.
